CAMPUS Datasheet Automotive OEM Datasheet
This datasheet of Ultraform® N2640 Z2 Q600 from BASF is provided by the international plastics database CAMPUS.

Ultraform® N2640 Z2 Q600 | (POM+PUR) | BASF
Product Texts
Elastomer-modified injection molding grade with high impact strength for clips, snap and fastening elements, and also for components subject to impact stress.

Abbreviated designation according to ISO 1043-1: POM-HI
Designation according to ISO 29988-POM-K,,M-GNPR,3-1
